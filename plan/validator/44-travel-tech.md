Frequently Asked Questions
How do I validate a TravelTech startup idea?
IdeaProof validates TravelTech with analysis of: travel market dynamics, booking behavior, seasonal demand, supplier relationships, and competitive landscape.

What makes a successful TravelTech startup?
Successful TravelTech: solves real traveler pain points, provides seamless booking, builds strong supplier relationships, handles seasonality, differentiates from OTAs.

What TravelTech categories are supported?
IdeaProof supports: flight booking, hotel booking, vacation rentals, travel planning, tour experiences, corporate travel, travel insurance, and destination guides.

How does IdeaProof handle TravelTech seasonality?
We analyze seasonal demand patterns, peak vs off-season economics, cash flow implications, marketing timing, and revenue optimization strategies.

What are TravelTech red flags?
Red flags: Competing directly with OTAs, thin margins, seasonal dependency, complex supplier integrations, high CAC, disintermediation risk.

How do I differentiate from Booking.com, Expedia?
Strategies: Niche focus (adventure, luxury, family), unique inventory, superior experience, B2B focus (corporate), vertical integration, loyalty/rewards.

What is the global TravelTech market size?
Global travel market recovered to $1.9 trillion in 2023 (95% of pre-pandemic). Online travel booking: $755 billion. OTA share: 40%.

What's the average commission for travel bookings?
Hotels: 15-25%. Flights: 1-3% (plus GDS fees). Tours/experiences: 20-30%. Car rentals: 8-15%. Vacation rentals: 3-15% (split host/guest).

How does seasonality impact TravelTech?
Peak seasons can be 3-5x off-season volume. Cash flow planning is critical. Marketing costs spike 50-100% in peak. Consider southern hemisphere for balance.

What supplier integrations are needed?
GDS (Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport) for flights. Channel managers for hotels. XML APIs for car rentals. Integration costs: $50K-$200K typically.

Which TravelTech segments grow fastest?
Fastest growing: Experiences/tours (25% CAGR), Sustainable travel (30%), B2B travel management (18%), Travel fintech (22%), and AI trip planning (40%).

What's typical CAC for travel startups?
B2C travel: $30-$100 CAC. B2B corporate: $500-$2000. Luxury travel: $100-$300. Metasearch: $5-$20 (but lower conversion). LTV:CAC should exceed 3:1.
