Micro-SaaS businesses are perfect for solo founders who want to build sustainable, profitable software without venture capital or large teams. These 52 micro-SaaS ideas focus on solving specific problems with simple, focused solutions that can be built and maintained by one person.

The best micro-SaaS ideas share common traits: they solve a painful problem for a specific audience, require minimal ongoing maintenance, and can grow through word-of-mouth. Focus on niches where you have domain expertise or personal experience with the problem. Many solo founders reach $10K-$100K ARR with these focused tools.

Related concepts: indie hacker, bootstrapped SaaS, solo founder, passive income software, lifestyle business, one person startup, saas for solopreneurs.
