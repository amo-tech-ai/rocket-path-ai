# Research Execution Guide

Systematic web research workflow for validating startup ideas. Use alongside the 9-dimension scorecard.

## Research Plan

Before searching, identify queries needed across 5 dimensions:
1. Market opportunity (TAM/SAM/growth)
2. Competitive landscape (direct, indirect, adjacent)
3. Problem validation (pain points, behavior, spend)
4. Trends (technology, regulatory, investment)
5. Business model (pricing, unit economics, channels)

**Minimum 10-15 web searches** for a thorough analysis.

## Search Query Templates

### Market Size (3-5 searches)
- "[industry] market size [year]"
- "global [product/service] market forecast"
- "[industry] CAGR growth rate"
- "number of [target customers] worldwide"

### Competitive Landscape (3-5 searches)
- "[product/service] alternatives"
- "best [product category] [year]"
- "[industry] startups funding"
- "[company] competitors"

### Problem Validation (2-3 searches)
- "[target audience] pain points [industry]"
- "why do [target audience] need [solution type]"
- "[problem] statistics"

### Trends (2-3 searches)
- "[industry] trends [year]"
- "future of [technology/market]"
- "[industry] investment report [year]"

### Business Model (1-2 searches)
- "[product] pricing models"
- "[industry] average customer acquisition cost"

## Data Sources (by quality)

### Tier 1: Primary Sources
Government data (Census, BLS), company reports, original research

### Tier 2: Analyst Reports
Gartner, Forrester, McKinsey, Statista, IDC

### Tier 3: Industry Press
TechCrunch, VentureBeat, trade publications

### Tier 4: Financial Data
Crunchbase, PitchBook, CB Insights, SEC filings

### Tier 5: Aggregators
Secondary analysis, blog posts (use cautiously)

## Source Quality Rules

- Cross-reference from at least 3 sources
- Prioritize data < 2 years old
- Note methodology differences
- Flag: no cited sources, promotional content, suspiciously precise forecasts

## Report Template

```markdown
# [Startup Idea] Validation Report

## Executive Summary
- One-paragraph overview
- Verdict: GO / CONDITIONAL / PIVOT / NO-GO
- 3-5 key findings

## Market Analysis
### Market Size & Growth
- TAM/SAM/SOM estimates with sources
- Growth rate and trajectory

### Market Trends
- Favorable trends
- Potential headwinds

## Competitive Landscape
### Direct Competitors
- List with descriptions, market position, strengths/weaknesses

### Indirect Competition
- Alternative solutions and substitutes

### Competitive Gaps
- Unmet needs and positioning opportunities

## Problem-Solution Fit
### Problem Validation
- Evidence, frequency, intensity
- Current solutions and limitations

### Solution Differentiation
- UVP, competitive advantages, potential moats

## Business Model Assessment
- Revenue model alignment
- Unit economics potential
- Customer acquisition channels

## Risk Analysis
### Critical Risks
- Deal-breakers and major challenges

### Manageable Risks
- Addressable concerns with mitigation strategies

## Positioning Recommendations
- Target market segment
- Value proposition
- Go-to-market strategy

## Validation Next Steps
1. Immediate actions
2. Customer interviews needed
3. MVPs to test
4. Metrics to track

## Sources
[All sources with links and dates]
```
