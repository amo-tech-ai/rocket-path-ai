## Quick scan checklist (monthly)

- [ ] **News:** TechCrunch, Forbes AI 50, Sifted AI 100 — who’s new?
- [ ] **Accelerators:** YC AI, Techstars — new batches / companies
- [ ] **Directories:** CB Insights report, F6S AI, TopStartups.io AI — overlap & newcomers
- [ ] **Lists:** All In, N47, HI.VC, Beauhurst — cross-check names
- [ ] **Research:** Stanford HAI Index, a16z — narrative and macro

---

## Format notes for research

- **Scan (breadth):** Use sections 1, 3, 5 first.
- **Validate (signal):** Use section 2 + investor links in section 4.
- **Classify:** Tag by product type (Copilot / Agent / Vertical SaaS / Infra), industry, GTM use case.
- **Synthesize:** Count mentions, build rankings and category heatmaps from section 5 + directory filters.
