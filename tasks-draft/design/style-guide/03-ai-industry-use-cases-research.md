# How AI Is Used Across Industries – Real Cases & Stats

## 1. Fashion / Apparel

**Summary:**
- AI adoption high in GEO, visual search, virtual try-on; 65% of fashion execs use GenAI regularly (FTBEC 2024)
- Shopping-related GenAI searches grew 4,700% Jul 2024–Jul 2025; 41% of consumers trust GenAI more than ads (BoF 2025)
- Main advantage: discovery, conversion, and returns/fraud; AI-driven revenue per visit +84% for leaders (SimilarWeb/BoF)
