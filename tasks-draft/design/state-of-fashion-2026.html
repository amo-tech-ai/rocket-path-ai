§1 · Tariff Turbulence
US tariffs spiked from 13% to 54% in three months — reshaping global fashion supply chains overnight.

In a flat market, only brands that capture hearts and minds will grow.
Ten themes converge on one reality: fashion must simultaneously navigate tariff turbulence (13% → 54% → 36%), AI transformation (4,700% shopping search growth, GEO as new SEO), consumer identity shifts (wellbeing as the new luxury, self-expression over status), and a luxury recalibration (9 of 15 brands reset, 80% growth was price-driven).

Meanwhile, mid-market elevation ($317B resale by 2027, COS bridging mass and luxury) and new growth frontiers ($460B jewellery, 2M+ smart glasses) are reshaping where value is created.

"Challenging" has overtaken "uncertainty" as the word executives most frequently use. How you respond determines which side of the disruption you're on.
