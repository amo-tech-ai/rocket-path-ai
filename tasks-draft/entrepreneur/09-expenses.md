## FAQ

**How do expenses link to other sections?**  
Expense forecasts connect with sales and financial projections, forming the backbone of budgeting and funding strategy.

**Can I customize categories?**  
Yes. Adjust COGS, Admin, CAPEX, and Borrowings to match your structure and operations.

**Do I need accounting experience?**  
No. PrometAI calculators are built for founders; structure costs without financial expertise.

**Why is structured expense planning important?**  
Accurate startup expenses give a realistic view of funding needs, avoid surprises, and make your business plan credible.

**How to calculate business startup costs?**  
List all expenses by category (COGS, Admin, CAPEX, Borrowings), estimate realistically. PrometAI helps create precise estimates aligned with budget and funding strategy.

**What are startup expenses?**  
Initial costs needed to launch and operate: setup fees, equipment, technology, marketing, staffing—everything that turns an idea into a functioning business.
