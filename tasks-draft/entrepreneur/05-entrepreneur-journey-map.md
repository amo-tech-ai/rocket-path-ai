## From Idea to Success – 5 Phases

### 1. Business Ideation & Development

- **Idea Generation & Validation** – Test concept, improve it, build a strong base
- **Founder Support** – Equity decisions, networking, Founder's Journey
- **Brand Identity & Design** – Name, logo, full visual identity

### 2. Legal & Administrative Setup

- **Business Registration** – Tax setup, legal formation, documentation
- **Human Resources & Team Building** – Hiring plans, compensation tools
- **Risk Management** – Financial, legal, operational risks; prevention

### 3. Marketing & Customer Acquisition

- **Marketing & SEO Strategies** – Targeted ads, social campaigns, email marketing
- **Sales & Revenue Models** – Pricing strategies, income forecasts
- **Task & Project Management** – Organized workflows

### 4. Financial Planning & Business Growth

- **Business Plan & Pitch Deck Generation** – AI tools, investor-ready plans
- **Valuation & Financial Projections** – Worth and trajectory
- **Expense Management** – Spending tracking, capital decisions

### 5. Essential Business Tools & Resources

- **AI-Driven Generators** – Ideas, logos, color palettes
- **Legal Document Templates** – Contracts, partnership agreements, compliance docs
- **Investor & Growth Support** – Funding resources, scaling, partnerships

## FAQ

**How to start the entrepreneur journey?**  
Define your idea, conduct market research, secure funding, register legally, build a brand, launch marketing. PrometAI simplifies each step.

**What are the 4 types of entrepreneurs?**
1. Innovative – Groundbreaking ideas  
2. Imitative – Adapt successful models  
3. Social – Social impact driven  
4. Lifestyle – Work-life balance and passion  

**What are the 7 steps of the entrepreneurial process?**
1. Idea Generation  
2. Business Planning  
3. Funding  
4. Legal Registration  
5. Marketing & Branding  
6. Operations & Execution  
7. Scaling & Expansion
