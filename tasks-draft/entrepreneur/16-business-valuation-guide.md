## Summary

Business valuation determines economic value for selling, investors, or M&A. PrometAI offers five methods: Cost of Capital (with startup adjustments), Estimation of Cost of Capital (WACC), Multiple-Based, DCF, and Asset-Based. Each provides a unique lens; PrometAI simplifies and automates the process.

## Core Components of Valuation

- **Assets** – Physical and intangible resources
- **Earnings** – Profitability over time
- **Market position** – Relative to competitors

## Five Valuation Methods

### 1. Cost of Capital Methodology

For startups; adjusts mature-firm baseline for illiquidity and survival risk.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| Base | Estimate cost of capital for mature firm in same industry |
| Illiquidity | Add premium for startup size/stage |
| Survival | Adjust by historical startup survival rate |

*Example (TechInnovate):* Base 10% + illiquidity 5% = 15%; with 50% survival rate → 7.5% adjusted.

### 2. Estimation of Cost of Capital (WACC)

| Component | Method |
|-----------|--------|
| **Cost of Equity** | CAPM: risk-free rate + (beta × market risk premium) |
| **Cost of Debt** | Interest rate on loans/bonds, adjusted for tax |
| **WACC** | Weighted average of equity and debt by capital structure |

*Example (EcoFurnishings):* Equity 9.2%, debt 3.5%; 60/40 structure → WACC 6.92%.

### 3. Multiple-Based Valuation

| Multiple | Compares |
|----------|----------|
| **P/E** | Share price to earnings per share |
| **EV/EBITDA** | Enterprise value to EBITDA |
| **P/S** | Market price to sales |

*Example:* P/E 25 vs 15 in same industry → potential undervaluation.

### 4. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Project future cash flows |
| 2 | Select discount rate (WACC) |
| 3 | Discount to present value |

*Example (GreenTech):* Cash flows $1M–$2M over 5 years; WACC 8% → present value.

### 5. Asset-Based Valuation

Net asset value = (Tangible + Intangible assets) − Liabilities.

*Example:* $5M tangible + $2M intangible − $3M liabilities = $4M net asset value.
