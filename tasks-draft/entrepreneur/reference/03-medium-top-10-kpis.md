# The Top 10 KPIs Every Startup Needs to Track

**Source:** [Medium – Sriram Chidambaram / Spice Route Finance](https://medium.com/@sriram_47381/the-top-10-kpis-every-startup-needs-to-track-cfo-services-strategy-deal-advisory-4f31320966)  
**Topic:** CFO perspective on universal startup KPIs

---

## Summary

Some KPIs are universal for startups across industries. Before choosing KPIs, apply four principles: measurable, aligned to goals, owned by leadership, and time-bound. Sriram Chidambaram outlines 10 KPIs applicable to most startups in consumer and enterprise technology.

---

## Guiding Principles for Meaningful KPIs

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Measurable** | Articulate success in numbers; trackable in real-time; ideally in a dashboard |
| **Aligned to business goals** | Reflect business focus and key functions; address pain points |
| **Owned** | Someone accountable for monitoring, interpreting, and optimizing each KPI |
| **Time-bound** | Specify timeline (e.g. "Reach X users by December 2020"); eliminate ambiguity |

---

## The Top 10 KPIs

| # | KPI | What it measures |
|---|-----|------------------|
| 1 | **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** | Cost to acquire a new customer; marketing + sales ÷ customers acquired |
| 2 | **LTV (Customer Lifetime Value)** | Value each customer brings over the relationship; repeat business; sustainability (CAC/LTV ratio) |
| 3 | **Return on CAC** | Time for customer to generate enough net revenue to recover acquisition cost; impacts cash flow |
| 4 | **Customer Retention Rate** | % retained over a period; (customers at end − new) ÷ customers at start |
| 5 | **Customer Churn Rate** | % lost over a period; inverse of retention |
| 6 | **Gross Margin** | Difference between production costs and revenues; includes CAC, dev, overheads; used for EBITDA |
| 7 | **Burn Rate** | Rate of cash spend; critical for early-stage; positive burn = spending more than earning |
| 8 | **Runway** | Time before running out of money; cash position ÷ monthly burn |
| 9 | **Revenue Growth** | Demand and strategy effectiveness; use CAGR for semi-annual/annual |
| 10 | **Operational Costs as % of Revenue** | Cost by function (sales, marketing, R&D, HR, IT, admin); value of each function; adjustable (e.g. in recession) |

---

## Key Insights

- **Burn rate:** Most startups fail from running out of cash; becoming cash-flow positive should be #1 priority for early-stage
- **CAC/LTV:** One of the most important sustainability indicators
- **Runway:** Startups heavy on marketing, ads, discounting have shorter runway; must secure financing or perish
