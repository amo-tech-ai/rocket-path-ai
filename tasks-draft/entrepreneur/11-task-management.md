## Task Management Beyond To-Do Lists

To-do lists capture actions but rarely create structure. Real progress comes from tools that connect planning, collaboration, and accountability.

Modern task management goes beyond reminders or checkboxes. It turns projects into systems of priorities, dependencies, and outcomes—linking milestones to timelines, tracking performance in real time, and aligning goals with the business roadmap.

**How does this connect to other sections?**
