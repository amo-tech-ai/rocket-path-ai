## Approach: Top-Down + TAM-Based

- **Top-down:** Estimate sales from total market potential (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- **Bottom-up:** Price × Units for operational capacity
- **Combined:** Balances market potential with realistic execution

---

## How to Forecast Sales for a Startup

1. **Define assumptions first** – Customer segments, pricing logic, key growth channels
2. **Use structured thinking** – Root forecasts in real market behavior, not optimism
3. **Combine top-down and bottom-up** – Market potential + operational capacity
4. **Test against benchmarks** – Seasonality, churn, acquisition costs
5. **Iterate** – Review monthly, compare predicted vs actual, adjust marketing, hiring, pricing

> A strong forecast never stands still. Let the data guide course corrections.
