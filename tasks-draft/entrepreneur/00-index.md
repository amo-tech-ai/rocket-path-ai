## Journey Flow

1. **Generate** – Creativity boosters, frameworks, case studies → structured idea
2. **Refine** – Audience, problem, value proposition → clear opportunity
3. **Validate** – Demand, profitability, risks → confidence before committing
4. **Benchmark** – Industry metrics and comparative analysis → grounded strategy
5. **Risks & Strategy** – Directional, performance, contextual risks → resilient execution
