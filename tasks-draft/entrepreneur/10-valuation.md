## FAQ

**Does the system support multiple scenarios?**  
Yes. Model different growth rates, cost structures, or capital assumptions to test impact on valuation and final value.

**Can I export valuation results?**  
Yes. PAT reports and valuation summaries can be exported for investor presentations, financial docs, or internal analysis.

**Do I need finance expertise?**  
No. The platform automates key calculations (cost of capital, DCF), guiding you through inputs and assumptions.

**Is DCF suitable for startups?**  
Yes. DCF estimates value from future cash flows and cost of capital, giving a transparent, data-backed view of growth potential.

**Why use PAT instead of just revenue?**  
Revenue alone doesn't show financial health. PAT provides a clearer view of actual profitability for planning and valuation.

**How do business owners profit?**  
When revenue exceeds expenses after taxes and capital costs. PAT can be reinvested or distributed as dividends or equity returns.

**How to calculate startup valuation?**  
Best done through DCF: discount future cash flows to present value using cost of capital. Captures growth potential, risk, and profitability in one model.
