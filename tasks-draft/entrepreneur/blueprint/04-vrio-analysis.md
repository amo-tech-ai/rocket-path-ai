# VRIO Analysis

**Source:** [PrometAI Blueprint – VRIO](https://prometai.app/blueprint/vrio-analysis)  
**Topic:** Resources and capabilities for sustainable competitive advantage

---

## Summary

**VRIO** = **Value**, **Rarity**, **Imitability**, **Organization**. A strategic framework to analyze a company's resources and capabilities and determine potential for sustainable competitive advantage. Resources that meet all four criteria provide a durable competitive edge.

---

## Four Questions (VRIO)

| Criterion | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| **Value** | Do the resources exploit opportunities or neutralize threats? |
| **Rarity** | Are these resources unique compared to competitors? |
| **Imitability** | Can competitors easily replicate these resources? |
| **Organization** | Is the company structured to capitalize on these resources? |

---

## How to Use VRIO

1. **Identify key resources** – List significant organizational resources and capabilities
2. **Evaluate value** – Does each resource exploit opportunities or neutralize threats?
3. **Assess rarity** – How unique vs competitors?
4. **Analyze imitability** – How difficult for competitors to replicate?
5. **Check organization** – Does structure support strategic use?

---

## VRIO vs SWOT

| VRIO | SWOT |
|------|------|
| Focus on internal resources for sustained advantage | Broader: internal + external |
| Deep dive on specific resources | General overview |
| Objective: long-term competitive edge | Objective: immediate strategic position |

---

## Benefits

- Clear competitive analysis – which resources drive advantage
- Resource optimization – allocate efforts to valuable resources
- Enhanced strategic planning – prioritize capability-building
- Improved sustainability – focus on hard-to-imitate resources
- Organizational alignment – structure supports strategy

---

## Example: PrometAI

AI-powered financial analysis platform:  
**Valuable:** Enhances financial decision-making  
**Rare:** Advanced AI, user-friendly interface  
**Imitable:** Sophisticated tech not easily replicated  
**Organized:** Skilled team, strategic partnerships  

→ Critical source of sustained competitive advantage.
