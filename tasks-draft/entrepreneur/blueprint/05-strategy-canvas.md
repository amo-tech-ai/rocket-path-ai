# Strategy Canvas

**Source:** [PrometAI Blueprint – Strategy Canvas](https://prometai.app/blueprint/strategy-canvas)  
**Topic:** Visualizing competitive landscape and Blue Ocean opportunities

---

## Summary

**Strategy Canvas** visualizes a company's competitive landscape. It plots key industry factors to show competition levels, revealing opportunities for new market spaces (Blue Oceans). Emphasizes under-served areas (e.g. user-friendliness, customer support) and shifts focus from direct competition to value innovation.

---

## Key Points

- Represents company position relative to competitors
- Helps set industry standards and differentiate
- Provides framework for continuous analysis and adaptation
- Highlights over-invested or under-invested areas
- Encourages creating new demand and Blue Oceans (uncontested markets)

---

## How to Create a Strategy Canvas

1. **Identify key industry factors** – List factors the industry competes on
2. **Gather data** – Your company and competitors for these factors
3. **Plot on template** – Visualize competition levels
4. **Analyze** – Identify strengths, weaknesses, over/under-invested areas
5. **Find value innovation opportunities** – Focus on under-served areas
6. **Create Blue Oceans** – Shift from competition to innovation

---

## Example: Fintech Industry

**Key factors:** User-friendliness, AI sophistication, Price, Customer support, Data security

**Analysis:** Competitors focus on AI and data security. Opportunity: emphasize user-friendliness and superior customer support.

**Result:** New market space for differentiation and strategic growth.

---

## Blue Ocean Strategy

**Blue Oceans** = Uncontested markets, ideal for growth. The framework encourages creating new demand rather than fighting in saturated Red Oceans.

---

## FAQ

**Strategy Canvas vs Strategy Map?** Canvas visualizes competitive factors and company performance; Strategy Map visualizes strategic goals and relationships within the organization.

**How to build?** Identify factors, gather competitor data, plot on template, analyze for innovation opportunities.

**How often to update?** Regularly to stay responsive to market changes.
