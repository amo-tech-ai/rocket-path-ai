# SWOT Analysis

**Source:** [PrometAI Blueprint – SWOT](https://prometai.app/blueprint/swot-analysis)  
**Topic:** Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

---

## Summary

**SWOT** = **Strengths**, **Weaknesses**, **Opportunities**, **Threats**. A framework to analyze what a company does best now and what it needs for future success. **Strengths** and **Weaknesses** are internal (resources, capabilities, processes). **Opportunities** and **Threats** are external (market, competition, economy).

---

## Four Parts of SWOT

| Element | Type | Focus |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Strengths** | Internal | Core capabilities and resources enhancing success |
| **Weaknesses** | Internal | Limitations impacting operational effectiveness |
| **Opportunities** | External | Trends or changes that can be leveraged for growth |
| **Threats** | External | Risks posing challenges to stability and growth |

---

## Primary Insights

- Structured approach to evaluating a business
- Identifies strategic advantages and vulnerabilities
- Encourages proactive thinking and strategic planning
- Supports clearer decision-making
- Essential for adapting to changing market conditions

---

## Example: PrometAI SWOT

**Strengths:** Robust AI platform; skilled team; strong brand in fintech  
**Weaknesses:** Intense tech competition; constant need for updates  
**Opportunities:** Emerging markets; strategic partnerships; AI demand in finance  
**Threats:** Evolving tech standards; regulatory changes; new entrants  

---

## FAQ

**What does SWOT stand for?** Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats.

**Why is SWOT important?** Provides critical insights for strategic decision-making and identifies areas for growth and improvement.

**How to write a SWOT analysis?** List Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats in a structured format.

**How often to conduct SWOT?** Regularly, especially before strategic planning or when entering new markets.

**What is the purpose?** Identify internal and external factors that impact business success, guiding strategic planning.
