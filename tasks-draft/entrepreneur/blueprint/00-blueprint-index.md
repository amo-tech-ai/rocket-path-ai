# Entrepreneur's Business Plan Blueprint

**Source:** [PrometAI Blueprint](https://prometai.app/blueprint)  
**Topic:** Structured framework for professional, investor-ready business plans

---

## Summary

The business plan blueprint is a structured framework for organizing company strategy. It outlines essential sections for a professional business plan: market analysis, financial forecasting, operational roadmaps. Designed to work with PrometAI’s software, it helps convert ideas into a finalized, investor-ready document.

---

## Blueprint Sections

| Section | Focus |
|---------|-------|
| **Descriptive** | Mission, Vision, Sensitivity Analysis |
| **Strategy Assessment** | SWOT, PESTEL, Porter's, VRIO, Business Phases, Stakeholders, Pain Points, Strategy Canvas, Roadmap, Allocation of Funds |
| **Marketing** | Competitive Advantage, Marketing Strategy, Target Market, Competitive Analysis |
| **Market Estimation** | Market Overview, Target Audience, SAM/TAM/SOM, Product Pricing |
| **HR** | Organizational Structure, Founder Team |
| **Tasks Planning** | General Tasks, Marketing Tasks, Business Development Phase Tasks |
| **Risks** | Stress Testing, Operational, Regulatory, Strategic, Finance, External Risks |
| **Financials** | Revenue/COGS Narratives & Calculations, SG&A, Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, CAPEX, Loans |
| **Valuation** | Cost of Capital, DCF, Multiple-Based, Asset-Based Valuation |
| **Appendix** | Glossary, Disclaimer |
