## SECTION: What Shoppers Need Before Trusting AI

**Visual: Trust checklist (ranked)**

```
#1  🔒  Strong data privacy         "How is my data protected?"
#2  ⏸️  Easy way to turn it off      "Can I stop this anytime?"
#3  ✅  Approval before buying       "Don't spend my money without asking"
#4  👁️  Transparency on data use     "What do you know about me?"
#5  🧑  Human backup available       "Can I talk to a person?"
```

**Context (2 sentences):**
Even Gen Z — the most AI-comfortable generation — only 63% want AI agents to buy for them. Until retailers address all 5 trust requirements, AI shopping stays a demo.
